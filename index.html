Our Approach

Detection

Use a Deep learning model to on high-resolution images of roads captured by a camera. Identify the pixels that belong to different types of road hazards, such as potholes, water stagnation, garbage, etc.

Information gathering

Extract the coordinates and dimensions of the detected road hazards from the segmented images. Store the images and the metadata in a database along with the location information obtained from GPS or other sources.

Solution

Send an alert to the municipal authorities with the details of the road hazards and their locations. Provide a web interface or a mobile app for the authorities to access the database and monitor the status of the road hazards.
